Bookings

Amusements of America offers your organization the opportunity to work in partnership with a proven, time-tested fundraiser. We service domestic markets across Texas.

Over 20 years of experience helping charitable and civic organizations reach their goals has given us the insight needed to plan and execute successful events. Your organizations need only provide permission for the use of a suitable location with access to a water source. Following this, it becomes a turnkey operation for you. We supply everything. From liability insurance naming your organization and any other parties involved, to the necessary permits. From amusement rides, games, and food, to the experienced personnel required for their operation. From cleanup and crowd control to marketing support, we do it all!
