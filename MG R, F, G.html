Rides? Food? Games?! Oh My?!

Rides Available

Texas Mardi Gras is renowned for it's thrilling roller coasters and rides. These machines were made especially for those true daredevil, adrenaline-junkies. The nation-known carnival rides, bound to bring forward everyone's inner child for a thrilling day, include:

  • Alien Abduction
  • ATVs
  • Baja Buggies
  • Balloon Wheel
  • Carousel
  • Cliff Hanger
  • Crystal Lils
  • Storm
  • Tornado

Food Available

The one thing Lousiana is nationally known for it's delicious, spicy seafood. From crawfish, to shrimp to gumbo, we replicate their tastebud-tingling recipes. These delicious southern-styled food guranteed to get your stomach growling at its mention and have you comming back for more after one bite, include:

  • Stovetop Crawfish Boil
  • Shrimp Malacca (with Rice)
  • Chicken-Andouille Gumbo
  • Crawfish Étouffée
  • Andouille Sasuage Jambalaya (with Shrimp)
  • New Orleans Beignets
  • Crawfish Bisque
  • Mini Muffulettas
  • Red Beans-and-Rice Veggie Burgers

Games Available

If you are not much of an adventure-seeker or into southern styled food is not for you, no worries! The Texas Mardi Gras Carnival has an abundance of booths and games to win amazing prizes and aaccolades. These family-fun games include:

  • Musical Carnival Caps
  • Mock Throw Up
  • Spoon the Balloon
  • Lucky Dip
  • High Striker
  • Crab Toss
  • Cork Gun Shoot
  • Guessing Booth
